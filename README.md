# 📊 Superstore Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Sales Performance Dashboard developed in Microsoft Power BI using the Superstore dataset.

The dashboard enables business managers and stakeholders to monitor sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and regional trends for better decision-making.

## Business Problem

Organizations generate large amounts of sales data every day, but without proper visualization it becomes difficult to identify:

- Top-performing states
- Best-selling products
- Customer segments driving revenue
- Profitability trends
- Sales growth over time

This dashboard transforms raw sales data into actionable business insights.

## Key Business Insights

### Overall Performance

- Revenue reached **$2.30M**
- Profit totaled **$286.40K**
- Profit Margin stood at **12.47%**
- Nearly **10,000 orders** were completed.

### Category Analysis

Technology generated the highest revenue.

Furniture ranked second.

Office Supplies maintained consistent sales volume.

### Customer Segments

Consumer customers generated over **50%** of total revenue.

Corporate customers contributed around **31%**.

Home Office represented approximately **19%**.

### Geographic Performance

California generated the highest sales.

New York and Texas followed as strong-performing states.

### Product Performance

Phones produced the highest revenue among all sub-categories.

Chairs and Storage also performed strongly.

### Shipping

Standard Class handled the majority of customer orders.

---

## Recommendations

- Increase investment in Technology products.
- Expand marketing campaigns targeting Consumer customers.
- Replicate California's successful sales strategies in other states.
- Improve profitability for lower-performing states.
- Encourage faster shipping options for high-value customers.
- Optimize inventory for top-selling sub-categories.
